Meet the Founder

Christa Pecha

CEO & Pet Care Professional

Born and raised in Los Angeles, I've always been surrounded by animals of all kinds — cats, dogs, rabbits, hamsters, and even snakes. My love for animals started at a young age and has only grown stronger over the years.

Wiggle Your Tail Pet Care was founded in Los Angeles with a simple goal: to provide loving, dependable care for pets and peace of mind for their families. From those early days, our commitment to treating every pet like family has remained at the heart of everything we do.

Now based in Sarasota, Florida, Wiggle Your Tail continues to offer the same high-quality services that pet parents came to love in Los Angeles. Whether it's personalized walks or overnight care — we strive to ensure your furry companions are safe, happy, and well-cared for.
